Peanut Butter Bagel with Honey

One of my favorite meals to ever exist is an utterly simple plain bagel with Peanut butter and honey on top. The reason being that it is both nutritious and simple to make once you gather all of the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Plain Bagel
  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Toast bagel for 2 minutes
  2. Spread peanut butter evenly on both bagel halves
  3. Squirt honey on top of each bagel halve - to your liking
